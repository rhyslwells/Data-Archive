[[Elexon]] manages the **Balancing and Settlement Code (BSC)** and is responsible for settlement of imbalances.
## Role

- Calculates imbalance positions
- Determines the system price
- Settles payments between participants

## System Price

The **system price** is the imbalance price applied to participants whose actual positions differ from their contracted positions.
