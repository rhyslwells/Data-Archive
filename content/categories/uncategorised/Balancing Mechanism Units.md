# Balancing Mechanism Units (BMUs)

**Balancing Mechanism Units (BMUs)** are the entities that participate in the [[Balancing Mechanism]].

## Types

- Large generators
- Large industrial demand
- Aggregated [[demand response]]
- Storage assets
- Interconnectors

## Notes

- Smaller assets typically participate via aggregators
- Participation requires technical and market integration
