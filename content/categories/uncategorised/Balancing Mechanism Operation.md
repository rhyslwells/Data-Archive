The [[Balancing Mechanism]] operates continuously in real time after Gate Closure.

## Time Structure

- **Settlement Period (SP)**: 30 minutes
- Dispatch instructions: ~1-minute resolution
- Pricing granularity: 5-minute [[imbalance pricing]] blocks

## Decision Factors

[[National Grid ESO|NESO]] accepts actions based on:

- System imbalance
- Network constraints
- Cost
- Reserve requirements

## Typical Dispatch Actions

- Increasing generator output
- Reducing generator output
- Adjusting demand (up or down)
