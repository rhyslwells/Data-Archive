# Demand Response in Electricity Markets

Demand Response participates across multiple market layers.

## Examples

- Capacity Market
- [[Balancing Mechanism]]
- Frequency services (e.g. Dynamic Containment)
- Distribution flexibility tenders

## Role

DR provides flexible capacity and real-time adjustment capability within these markets.
