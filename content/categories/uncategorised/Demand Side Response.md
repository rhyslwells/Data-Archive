# Demand Side Response (DSR)

**Demand Side Response (DSR)** refers to mechanisms that incentivise consumers to adjust electricity usage.

## Actions

- Reduce demand
- Increase demand
- Shift demand in time

## Objective

- Modify the load profile to support:
  - System stability
  - Peak reduction
  - Cost efficiency

## Notes

DSR is often used interchangeably with [[Demand Response]], though usage can vary by context.
