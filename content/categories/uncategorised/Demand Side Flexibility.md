# Demand Side Flexibility

**Demand Side Flexibility (DSF)** refers to the ability of electricity demand to adjust in response to system needs.

## Actions

- Reduce consumption
- Increase consumption
- Shift consumption over time

## Role

- Supports real-time system balance
- Enables higher renewable penetration
- Provides services to:
  - Transmission system operators
  - Distribution network operators

## Relationship to DR

[[Demand Response]] is a mechanism used to realise demand side flexibility.
