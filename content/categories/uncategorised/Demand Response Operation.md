# Demand Response Operation

DR is used operationally to manage variability and system stress.

## Functions

- Reduce demand during peak periods
- Shift demand to periods of excess generation
- Support system stability

## Renewable Systems Context

- Solar generation creates midday surplus
- Net demand increases sharply in the evening

DR helps to:

- Shift load into surplus periods
- Reduce ramping requirements on generation

## Examples

- Smart EV charging
- Heat pump control
- Industrial load shifting
