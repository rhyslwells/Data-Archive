# Demand Response Economics

DR introduces price responsiveness into electricity demand.

If demand is $D(P)$, elasticity is:

$$
\epsilon = \frac{\partial D}{\partial P}
$$

## Effects

- Increases $|\epsilon|$ (demand becomes responsive)
- Reduces peak prices
- Lowers total system cost
- Reduces generator market power

## Interpretation

Without DR, demand is largely inelastic. DR enables demand to respond to market conditions, improving efficiency.
