# Demand Response Baselines

DR requires estimating a **baseline demand** to measure the impact of interventions.

$$
\Delta D(t) = D_{baseline}(t) - D_{actual}(t)
$$

## Purpose

- Quantify delivered demand reduction
- Enable financial settlement

## Challenges

- Baseline estimation error
- Potential for strategic manipulation

## Methods

- Historical averaging
- Regression models (weather, occupancy, production)
- Control groupsL
