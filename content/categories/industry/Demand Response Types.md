# Demand Response Types

Demand Response can be classified based on how signals are delivered.

## Price-Based DR

Consumption responds to electricity prices. Price-based DR is decentralised and continuous

Examples
- Time-of-Use (ToU)
- Critical Peak Pricing (CPP)
- Real-Time Pricing (RTP)

## Incentive-Based DR

Participants are paid to ==adjust demand== when instructed. Incentive-based DR is dispatch-driven and event-based

Examples
- Capacity market participation
- Balancing services
- Interruptible load contracts
