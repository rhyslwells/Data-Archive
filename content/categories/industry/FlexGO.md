# Note: **Flex Go** (Commercial Offering)

This note should describe the **customer-facing product**, not the market theory.

## Core Proposition

Flex Go is a simplified revenue-sharing product that enables businesses to monetise flexible electricity consumption or generation with minimal operational burden.

---

## Target Assets

* Refrigeration
* Pumps
* Heat pumps
* Batteries
* EV chargers
* Electrified heating

---

## Service Model

* Customer declares availability via portal
* No fixed forward commitment required
* Emphasis on ad hoc flexibility
* [[Flexitricity]] manages market optimisation and dispatch
* Compliance handled centrally

---

## Revenue Model

* Revenue stacking across markets
* Revenue backcast modelling for projections
* Incremental income from existing assets

---

## Strategic Framing

Flex Go operationalises energy flexibility in response to:

* Increased renewable penetration
* Growing volatility
* Rising system balancing needs
