### Piclo flex: platform:

**Role:**  
A digital marketplace where **network operators (DSOs/DNOs)** procure flexibility from providers.

**What it does:**

- Hosts tenders for flexibility services (e.g. reduce load, increase generation)
- Matches **buyers (e.g. UK Power Networks, SSEN)** with **sellers (aggregators, asset owners)**
- Standardises products, contracts, and bidding processes
- Provides transparency on location-specific grid constraints

**Core idea:**  
Piclo does not control assets. It enables others to **discover, bid, and transact**.
