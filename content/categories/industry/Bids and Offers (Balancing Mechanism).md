Participants submit prices for adjusting generation or demand.

## Definitions

- **Offer**: price to increase generation or reduce demand  
  $$\Delta P > 0$$

- **Bid**: price to decrease generation or increase demand  
  $$\Delta P < 0$$

## Characteristics

- Negative bids are common (e.g. wind curtailment)
- Prices reflect operational and opportunity costs

## Outcome

Accepted bids and offers result in instructed changes to generation or demand.
